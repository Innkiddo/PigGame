How To Play PigGame?

step 1.click on ROLL DICE to start the game.

step 2.click on HOLD to receive your score or click on ROLL DICE again to see more possible chances.

step 3.Try to reach '100' scores to win.(You can set winning score on FINAL SCORE field;otherwise the default is '100').

step 4.click on NEW GAME to reset the progress.

Note:if your dice shows '1' you will lose and your turn burns.
